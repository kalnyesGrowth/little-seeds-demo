18 Months – 2 Years
Toddler Classroom

Gentle, nurturing care in a safe and stimulating environment. Our caregivers follow individualized routines that support healthy development and strong early bonds.

Low caregiver-to-toddler ratio for hands-on attention

Daily parent communication on routines and milestones
Faith-integrated songs, stories, and calm routines

Preschool Classroom
