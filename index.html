Our Programs

Care & Education for
Every Stage of Childhood

From your baby's first steps to your big kid's after-school hours — Little Seeds of Hope Academy is built to grow with your family.

Infant · 12–18 Months
Little Sprouts

Gentle, nurturing care in a secure and stimulating environment. Our trained caregivers follow individualized routines to support healthy development from day one.

Low caregiver-to-infant ratio for hands-on attention
Sensory play and age-appropriate stimulation daily
Daily communication with parents on routines
Faith-integrated songs, stories, and calm routines
Toddler · 18 Mo – 3 Yrs
Growing Roots

Curious, active toddlers need structure and freedom in equal measure. Our toddler program blends guided exploration with the warmth of a faith-centered community.

Language development and early literacy activities
Structured play zones that develop motor skills
Biblical values woven into daily storytimes
Social-emotional learning and positive guidance
Pre-K · 3–5 Years
Blooming Learners

School readiness through hands-on learning. Our Pre-K program prepares children academically and emotionally for kindergarten — with joy, creativity, and purpose.

Kindergarten-readiness literacy & math skills
STEM exploration through creative play
Character development rooted in faith
Arts, music, and movement every day
After School · 5–12 Years
Rising Seeds

A safe, enriching place to land after the school day. Homework help, healthy snacks, outdoor play, and positive mentorship in a faith-filled environment.

Supervised homework time every afternoon
Outdoor and creative recreational activities
Healthy snacks provided daily
Mentorship and character-building activities
