HEALTH & SAFETY

Your Child's Safety Is Our First Priority

From the moment you drop off to the moment you pick up, every protocol is designed to keep your child safe, healthy, and smiling.

🏛️

Licensed by Texas HHSC

We meet all Texas Health and Human Services Commission standards for early childhood facilities — inspected and fully compliant.

🔒

Secure Single-Entry Access

Our facility uses controlled single-entry access. Only authorized adults may enter — no exceptions, no compromises.

✅

Background-Checked Staff

Every team member undergoes comprehensive background screening before their first day. CPR and First Aid certified for every class.

🌡️

Daily Health Screenings

Each child receives a wellness check at drop-off. We maintain clean, sanitized environments and follow strict illness exclusion protocols.
